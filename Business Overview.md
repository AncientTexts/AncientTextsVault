# Business Overview

## What We Do

Ancient Texts publishes AI-powered translations of public domain Latin texts as Kindle ebooks on Amazon KDP.

The pipeline takes Latin XML source files (from Perseus Digital Library), runs them through an LLM for translation and quality control, generates epub files with custom covers, and publishes them on Amazon KDP.

## Revenue Model

- Amazon KDP — 70% royalty tier on ebooks
- All titles enrolled in KDP Select

## Remuneration

- 15% of net revenue donated to Internet Archive or Perseus Project
- Renegotiation milestones: $100/mo → $500/mo → $1k/mo → $5k/mo recurring
